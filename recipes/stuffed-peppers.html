Stuffed Peppers

Description

Ingredients

  • 4 Bell Peppers
  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 1/2 cup Rice
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Can of Diced Tomatoes
  • 2 Tbsp Tomato Paste
  • 1 Can of Corn
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Cumin
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Cooking Oil

Steps

  1. Wash Bell Peppers and cut off tops/remove seeds inside
  2. Line a square baking pan with foil and spray with oil and then preheat oven to 400
  3. Place hollowed-out bell peppers into square pan
  4. Dice onion
  5. Heat small amount of oil and diced onion in a small pot until fragrant and then add water and heat to boil
  6. Add rice and cover then cook for approximately 20 min
  7. While rice is cooking, cook ground beef in medium-large pan over medium-high heat until browned
  8. Drain fat from pan once meat is cooked
  9. Add cooked rice to pan with ground beef, then add diced tomatoes, tomato paste, desired amount of corn, and the rest of the diced onion
  10. Season with garlic powder, onion powder, cumin, salt and pepper and stir until thoroughly mixed
  11. Fill the hollowed-out peppers with the mixture and then bake in the oven for 45 min or until desired tenderness is reached
  12. Remove from oven, sprinkle cheese on top of peppers and serve. Enjoy!
