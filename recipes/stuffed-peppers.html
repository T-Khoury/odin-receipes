Stuffed Peppers

Description

Ingredients

  • 4 Bell Peppers
  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 1/2 cup Rice
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Can of Diced Tomatoes
  • 2 Tbsp Tomato Paste
  • 1 Can of Corn
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Cumin
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Cooking Oil

Steps

  1. Wash Bell Peppers and cut off tops/remove seeds inside
  2. Line a square baking pan with foil and spray with oil and then preheat oven to 400
  3. Place hollowed-out bell peppers into square pan
  4. Dice onion
  5. Heat small amount of oil and diced onion in a small pot until fragrant and then add water and heat to boil
  6. Add rice and cover then cook for approximately 20 min
