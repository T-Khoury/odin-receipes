Air Fryer Quesadilla

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 Tortillas (taco size)
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Shredded Chicken
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Bell Pepper
  • Salsa
  • Sour Cream
  • Cooking Oil

Steps

  1. Cut Onion and Bell Pepper into Strips
  2. Sautee Onion and Pepper in a pan with a little bit of oil until slightly soft
  3. Preheat Air Fryer to 385
  4. Lay down 1 tortilla and sprinkle with cheese
  5. Add desired amount of shredded chicken and onion/peppers
  6. Sprinkle more cheese and place 2nd tortilla on top
  7. Use a fork to crimp the edges of the quesadilla together
  8. Lightly brush outside of tortillas with oil
  9. Cook in Air Fryer at 380 for 10 minutes, flipping the quesadilla halfway
  10. Remove from Air Fryer, cut into slices and serve with salsa and sour cream. Enjoy!
