Chicken Katsu

Crispy, yummy, and delicious!

Ingredients

  • Boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
  • All-purpose Flour
  • Panko bread crumbs
  • Egg
  • Oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Pound chicken breast halves to 1/2 inch thickness
  2. Beat egg
  3. Season chickn breasts on both sides with salt/pepper. Place flour, beaten egg, and pank crumbs into separate dishes
  4. For each piece of chicken, coat in flour, shake off excess, dip into egg, and then dip into panko crumbs on both sides
  5. Heat oil in large skillet over medium-high heat
  6. Fry chicken in hot oil until goldren brown, approximately 3-4 min per side
  7. Place chicken pieces on paper-towel lined plate to dry/cool
  8. Serve with rice and your favorite sauce and Enjoy!
